About Me

Hi! My name is Nigel Clayton, i'm 24 years old and live in Decatur GA.
I got into coding by taking a HTML elective class in my senior year of
highschool and thought it was very fun to learn. After highschool, I initially
decided to pursue a graphic design degree at Kennesaw State University before
changing it Game Design and Development in my first year there. During my 4 years
there leading to graduation, I learned a lot about Java, not really liking at first
but warmed up to it later, made board games before designing games in Unity with
C#, and learning time management and working with and communicating with a team to reach milestones.
After graduating in May of 2021 I started looking for a job but concluded that I needed
more experience in my field, so when I saw the description for Quotum Tech, I thought it was
a good opportunity to gain that experience. One of my goals beyond Quotum is to eventually
join or start a video game company.
